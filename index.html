Laptop Price Project

Project Overview

Welcome to our Laptop Price project! This data analysis project focuses on understanding and predicting laptop prices based on various specifications and features. We've analyzed a comprehensive dataset of laptops to identify key factors that influence pricing and created a prediction model to estimate laptop prices.

Our analysis includes data preprocessing, feature engineering, and the development of a linear regression model to predict laptop prices. We've also created various visualizations to help understand the relationships between different laptop specifications and their prices.

Data Introduction

The dataset used in this project focuses on laptop specifications and prices, with the source from Kaggle. It includes data such as brand, processor type, RAM, storage, and other features, along with the price of each laptop.

The prices in the dataset are listed in Indian Rupees (INR), reflecting the Indian market trends and consumer preferences. This data provides valuable insights into the affordability, market segmentation, and pricing strategies of laptops in India. By analyzing this dataset, we aim to uncover trends and relationships that can guide decision-making for both consumers and businesses.
